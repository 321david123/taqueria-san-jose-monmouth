Nuestra Taquería

Authentic tacos, the way a taquería should taste.

Taqueria San Jose is a neighborhood taquería on North Main Street, where the tortillas are fresh, the salsas are made in-house, and the tacos come the way they should — corn tortillas, plenty of cilantro and onion, a wedge of lime. Regulars come for the street tacos, the birria and the weekend menudo.

Order at the counter, grab a table, or pull up to the drive-through and take it to go. Guests keep it simple in their reviews — "delicious and affordable," "fresh tortillas and excellent salsas," "friendly, attentive and very quick." That's the whole idea.

Los Favoritos

The tacos people drive across town for

01

Street Tacos

The house standard — grilled meat on warm corn tortillas, finished with fresh cilantro, onion and a squeeze of lime. Order them asada, pastor, chicken or lengua. "Awesome street tacos," as the regulars put it.

Ask for asada · pastor · lengua On the menu →
02

Birria

Beef slow-cooked until it falls apart, tucked into tacos with onion and cilantro. Deep, rich and one of the reasons guests keep coming back — "really tasty and fair enough" for a full plate.

Tacos & plates See the plates →
03

Tortas & Burritos

When you want it bigger: a loaded torta or a fat burrito with rice, beans and your choice of meat. A local favorite — "I recommend the torta," say the regulars — and it travels great through the drive-through.

Hand-held & hearty Build yours →
04

Nachos & Cheese Dip

Crisp chips piled with melted cheese, meat and fresh pico. Start with the chips and salsa — "the cheese dip and salsa are very good" is about the most common thing you'll hear around here.
